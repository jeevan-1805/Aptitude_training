Training rhythm

Daily, weekly, monthly

Daily

1 concept block, 20–30 solved examples, 30–50 practice questions, and 10 minutes of error-log review.

Weekly

2 topic tests, 1 mixed test, and a revision session for formulas and common traps.

Monthly

1 full mock cycle with strict timing, followed by deep analysis of accuracy and speed.
